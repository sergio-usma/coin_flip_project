What is Heads or Tails?

Heads or tails is a game of chance in which a coin is used. Participants have to choose one side of the coin and toss it. The winner is whoever guesses which side of the coin will land heads up. This game has different names in other countries, such as face or shield, face or mint, face or seal, flying or eagle or sun.
